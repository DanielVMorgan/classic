This site was designed to evoke the look and feel of Classic Mac OS and HyperCard when the graphics were 1-bit black and white pixels. Most of the navigation icons—the pointing hands, home button, mail button, category icons—are vector recreations that will always appear as 1-bit icons at any size. I wrote the code over a four-day sprint and it is hosted at Github.

Super special tip of the hat to Susan Kare, for the original Mac icon look.
